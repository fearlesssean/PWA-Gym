How to Use PWA-Gym

Welcome to PWA-Gym! This app helps you track your workouts and follows progressive workout cycles.

1. Getting Started

  • On the Account page input and save your 1-RM for both bench and squat.
  • If you are unsure what your max should be, use the 1-RM calculator to help determine what it should be.

2. Navigation

  • Workouts: View and complete your workout programs
  • Logs: Track your workout history and progress
  • Account: Manage your personal settings
  • Info: Access app information and guidance

3. Proper Weights

Your 1RM may need to be adjusted as you progress through your training cycle. Use the following guidelines to determine adjustments based on your Week 1 performance:

  • If you achieve 8–9 reps on the last set, stick with your current 1RM—this indicates you're on track.
  • If you achieve only 6–7 reps, decrease your 1RM by 10 pounds for the next week's workout to maintain proper progression.
  • If you can complete 12 or more reps, increase your 1RM by 10 pounds for the next workout to continue challenging yourself.

These adjustments ensure your training intensity stays aligned with your goals.

4. Tips for Success

  • Follow the progressive cycle structure
  • Log your workouts consistently
  • Adjust weights as recommended
  • Focus on proper form over weight
